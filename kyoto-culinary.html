Kyoto, Japan: Hidden Culinary Journey

Duration: 4 Days

Price: $200

Dive deep into the culinary wonders of Kyoto on this four-day immersive journey. This adventure takes you off the beaten path to explore hidden culinary treasures, where you'll engage in hands-on experiences, taste exquisite local delicacies, and gain insider knowledge from seasoned local chefs and guides. Visit bustling food markets, take part in traditional cooking classes, and discover the secrets of Kyoto's cuisine, all while exploring the city's cultural gems. From early morning visits to sacred shrines to exclusive sake tastings in intimate settings, this journey provides an authentic and unforgettable look at Kyoto's vibrant food culture.

Back to Browse Adventures

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Begin your journey with an early morning visit to Fushimi Inari Shrine before the crowds arrive. Experience a guided tour through Nishiki Market, sampling street food and learning about Kyoto's culinary history. End the day with a dinner at a hidden, locally-recommended restaurant.
  • Day 2: Start with a traditional cooking class where you'll learn to make Kyoto-style dishes like tempura and miso soup. In the afternoon, explore the Gion district, followed by an exclusive sake tasting at a small sake bar only known to locals.
  • Day 3: Discover Kyoto's lesser-known temples, away from the usual tourist paths. Enjoy a tea ceremony experience at a family-run tea house. In the evening, participate in a mystery dining experience featuring a unique, surprise menu curated by a local chef.
  • Day 4: Spend your final day exploring Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and visit a local farm to learn about Kyoto's traditional farming methods. Finish with a scenic boat ride on the Hozu River, enjoying the natural beauty and reflecting on your culinary journey.
